Motor Vehicle In the Indian Market

Scooter

A Scooter is a two- wheeler motor vehicle with a step through frame and a platform for the rider's feet. It is Known for it's ease of use and fuel efficiency

Motorcycle

A Motorcycle is a two-wheeled motor vehicle with a more upright seating position and typically a manual transmission. It is popular for is performance and versatility.

Hatchback

A Hatchback is a type of car with a rear door that opens upwards, providing access to a cargo area. It is known for its compact size and practicality

Sedan

A sedan is a four -door pasanger car with a seperate trunk for cargo . it is often chosen for its comfortable and spacious interior.

SUV( Sports Utility vehicle)

An SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a larger vehicle designed for off-road capability and versatility. It is popular for its high driving position and ample cargo space.

MUV(Multi Utility Vehicle

An MUV is a vehicle designed to transport passengers and cargo. it often features a spaceous interior and is suitable for family use.

Electric Vehicle

An Electric vehicle is powered by one or more electric motors, using energy stored in rechargable batteries. It is chosen for its eco friendly and sustainable features.
